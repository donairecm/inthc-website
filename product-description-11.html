Description: A Napoleon III, gilded soft metal and blue porcelain mounted mantel clock. The circular porcelain dial painted with a band of flowers and roman numerals, inset a gilt case surmounted by a pair of cooing doves with nest, above a central panel of a classical maiden playing the triangle, flanked by musical trophies. The sides supported by Hermes, with a stepped plinth base with further porcelain panels. The Japy Freres & Cie eight day movement with Brocot suspension rack striking the hour & half hour on a bell. Movement & pendulum stamped 2493. Regilded.
